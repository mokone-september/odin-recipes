Easy Chicken Curry

Description

This easy chicken curry is a flavorful and comforting dish, perfect for weeknight dinners. Tender chicken pieces are simmered in a rich, aromatic sauce made with spices, tomatoes, and coconut milk.

Serve this curry with steamed rice or warm naan bread for a satisfying meal that everyone will love. It's simple to prepare and packed with delicious flavors!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (optional)
  • 1 (14 oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (14 oz) can coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish
  • Cooked rice or naan, for serving

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Stir in the curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder. Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned on all sides.
  5. Pour in the diced tomatoes and coconut milk. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer.
  6. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
