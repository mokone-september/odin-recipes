Fluffy Pancakes

Description

These fluffy pancakes are light, airy, and perfect for a delicious breakfast or brunch. They are easy to make and come out golden brown every time.

Serve them warm with your favorite toppings like maple syrup, fresh berries, or a pat of butter for a classic morning treat everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using about 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Brown on both sides and serve hot with your favorite toppings.
